Book Two · Discovery arc · The Pairing

Thread of Silver.

YA Fantasy· ~172K words· 27 chapters· Ages 14+

They came for Lira at the library. They came for Tieran an hour later. He doesn't know they have her. She doesn't know they have him. Both will, soon, in the most efficient way the Threadweavers know how to do it.

The interrogation is not about what Lira did. It is about what her thread is, and the people asking the questions already know more about her than she does. They will ask her to give it up. Tieran will be in the next room.

Threads can carry messages the system cannot read. Threads can also be cut. The book is about both.

"The thread did not snap. She made a choice, and the choice was the thing that frayed it."

Thread of Silver is Book Two of The Pairing. It is the book where Lira learns how the system actually works, and what it costs to know.
