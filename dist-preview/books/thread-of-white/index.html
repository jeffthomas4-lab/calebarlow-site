Book Three · Discovery arc · The Pairing

Thread of White.

YA Fantasy· ~199K words· 43 chapters· Ages 14+

Lira is a prisoner of the Threadweavers, and the thread they want is older than the institution holding her. The legend they pretend they don't believe in is sitting in a cell, breathing.

Tieran is leading the rescue. The Untied are with him. So are people he is not sure he can trust. The system is louder now and the system is also weaker than it has ever been, and both of those things are true at the same time.

The choice waiting at the end of this book is the one the entire series has been building toward. Lira can keep her bond and the system. Or she can break the bond and break the system. There is no version where she keeps both.

"The First Pair did not survive. Lira is paying attention to that."

Thread of White closes the Discovery arc of The Pairing and sets up the Rebellion arc that follows.
