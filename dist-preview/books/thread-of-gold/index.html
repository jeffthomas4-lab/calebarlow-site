Thread of Gold.

YA Fantasy· ~104K words· 33 chapters· Ages 14+

Lira sees threads. The colored bonds between paired people light up for her like nobody else in the city can manage, and she has been told her whole life to keep that quiet.

At thirteen she meets Tieran. Their threads tangle on contact. The color the bond turns is not a color the Threadweavers admit exists. White is a problem. White is a flag.

The Threadweavers say their job is to protect pairings. What Lira begins to see, once she knows where to look, is that protection is the wrong word. The threads are not being kept safe. They are being read.

"She is thirteen years old and she is already the variable they cannot solve for."

Thread of Gold is Book One of The Pairing, a 12-book YA fantasy series. It opens the Discovery arc and introduces a magic system, a romance, and a regime that has lasted because nobody has ever been born who could see what Lira sees.
